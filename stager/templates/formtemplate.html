Stager

This configuration is for an AMD64 system running systemd. It has two hard drives defined. The first disk has a GPT label. Its first partition will be formatted with fat32. The second partition will be formatted btrfs. The second disk also has a gpt label. It has one partition formatted with ext4 and will be mounted at /data1. Two users are added to the system at the end.
